The Product

Revenue Architecture

Features

The Arsenal

Every mechanic designed to maximize engagement, retention, and volume. Not a roadmap promise — the product spec.

P2PHigh Stakes

DUELS

Direct P2P confrontation. A player posts a duel order with a fixed price (e.g. Limit YES @ $0.50) and a side bank (e.g. 100K). Anyone can accept by taking the opposite outcome at equal size. Winner takes the entire bank on top of standard market payout.

StakingTM$

REWIND

Once per month, TM$ stakers can lock a “rewind point” on an active market. If the outcome goes fully wrong, the balance rolls back to the snapshot value. Protection size scales with stake amount and duration.

Hidden OddsHigh Risk

BLIND MARKETS

Markets with hidden probabilities. True position distribution stays invisible until the final phase. Current odds are revealed only at the moment of execution — full uncertainty until resolution.

10-15 minReal-time

FLASH MARKETS

Ultra-short markets that resolve in 10-15 minutes. High speed, high pressure. Built for users who want instant outcomes and rapid-fire decision-making.

TM$ ExclusiveAI

AI FORECAST

Exclusive to TM$ stakers. AI analyzes the order book, position dynamics, and participant activity to deliver a real-time probability forecast — your edge before the market moves.

SocialRevenue Share

SQUADS

Collective pools with a share (pae) system. Users enter with any amount and receive a proportional share of the pool. On exit, payouts are recalculated at current share value. Squad admins set their own management fee. Built for influencers and community leaders.

UGCFee Sharing

MARKET CREATION

Any user can create a market by posting a fixed deposit. After passing moderation, the creator earns a share of all trading fees generated by their market. User-generated content meets revenue sharing.

AIEducation

POST-RESOLVE INSIGHTS

After a market closes, AI publishes a breakdown: why the outcome unfolded, which signals pointed to it early, and how you could have adjusted your strategy. Learning built into the product.

TikTok FeedSwipe UX

SWIPE MARKETS

TikTok/Tinder-style interface: swipe through markets — right to go long, left to pass. Instant engagement without needing to study the order book. Discovery mode for the next generation of traders.

Real-timeAI

AI COMMENTATOR

Live AI commentary on market movement in real time — price dynamics, sentiment shifts, activity spikes. Like a sports commentator, but for prediction markets. Makes markets feel alive.

TournamentPrize Pool

LAST MAN STANDING

Tournament format: all participants predict, anyone who gets it wrong is eliminated. Round after round until one remains. The winner takes the entire prize pool. Spectacle-grade engagement.

NFT-likeIdentity

TRADING CARD

Your personal stats card — PnL, prediction accuracy, rare wins. Visually evolves over time as you level up. Designed for sharing and building reputation within the community.
